PAWS for a Paws-itive Cause

The Philippine Animal Welfare Society, PAWS for short, is a registered non-profit organization that is dedicated to the protection and promotion of humane treatment of all animals. I have chosen this charity organization because it promotes animal welfare, which is essential for protecting the animals found in our communities. So, why do I support this organization? Here are my top reasons:

  • To raise awareness of the proper treatment of animals, both domestic and strays, in our respective communities
  • Promote adoption of pets instead of purchasing, because some animals come from pet mills that have harsh conditions for animals
  • Show support for RA 8485, which is the Animal Welfare Act. It prevents cruelty, maltreatment, and neglect of animals, with penalties like imprisonment and fines for violations
